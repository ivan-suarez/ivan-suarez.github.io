Home

Technology does not just improve lives, it fundamentally reshapes them and sometimes not in a way we desire. The internet can be a virtual steppe of unlimited knowledge or an algorithm-driven perpetual scrolling prison. Facing this we have the choice to build tools to empower us as individuals and as a community, instead of trapping ourselves in an endless cycle of consumption and status seeking. We do not live in a deterministic world, but we are in front of multiple paths that we can choose to follow. In this website, I'm trying to envision the door that I want to open.
